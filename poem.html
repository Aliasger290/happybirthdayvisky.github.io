Here is a small poem for you...


I almost forgot your birthday again,
I was watching batman fight bane,
Luckily the mobile notification I didn’t ignore,
Hence Google calendar saved me once more,

Since its your birthday I sit here to write,
We don’t talk much so I hope you’re doing alright.
We’ve been through so much already,
Still a long way to go for life to be steady.

I hope today you enjoy yourself,
Keep all the worries in the back of the shelf.
Adulthood sucks I know,
But let’s take our own time to grow.

Even though I make you mad and you drive me nuts,
We will always have each other’s trust.
We laugh, we cry, we make time fly.
Together we smile, together we sigh.

We share our dreams, these desires that burn,
And someday we believe will be our turn!
Eight long years and we are still going strong
For the understanding we share in life's ping-pong.

We will reach our destination, our time will come,
In that journey we will leave our bread crumbs.
But for now, jump up high and touch the sky,
Let laughter soar and silliness fly.

Life gives you lessons to learn as you grow,
Room for mistakes and time to show what you know.
Just be patient because things will soon go your way.
Enjoy the young times and have a happy birthday!
